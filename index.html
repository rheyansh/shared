Privacy Policy for K Player

Effective Date: March 03, 2026

Welcome to K Player. Your privacy is our top priority. This policy outlines how we handle information in our offline music player application.

1. Absolute Data Privacy No Collection

We believe your music and your data belong to you. K Player does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

  • No Personal Info: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or device IDs.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies, web beacons, or third-party analytics (like Firebase or Google Analytics).
  • No Accounts: No sign-up or login is required to use any feature of the app.

2. App Permissions & Usage

To provide a high-performance music experience, the app requires specific permissions on your device:

  • Storage Access: Required to scan and play audio files stored locally on your device. We do not upload your files to any server.
  • Foreground Service: Required for the 3-Way Player Experience (Full, Mini, and Micro players) to ensure music continues playing while you use other apps or lock your screen.

3. The Offline Experience

K Player is designed as an offline tool. All features, including library management, smart playlists, and playback speed adjustments, are processed locally on your device. Any "Backup & Sync" features mentioned are strictly user-initiated and do not involve unauthorized data harvesting.

4. Ad-Free & Third-Party Policy

K Player is 100% Ad-Free. We do not share any information with third-party advertising networks because we do not collect any information to begin with.

5. Children’s Privacy

K Player does not collect any personal information and is fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). It is safe for use by individuals of all ages.

6. Compliance with Google Play

This policy meets the requirements of the Google Play Developer Distribution Agreement. As an offline utility, K Player ensures that user "Media and Files" are handled with the highest standard of local security.
